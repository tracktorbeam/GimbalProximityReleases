Get Gimbal up and running with your iOS App.

Adding a Framework/Library

Adding a Framework or Library to an XCode Project follows the same steps. Below is a pictorial 'how-to' for adding libz.dylib to your XCode project.

Adding libz.dylib to your project


Select your project's Application Target and navigate to the 'Summary' view.

Scroll down to the 'Linked Frameworks and Libraries' section and selct the '+' button. A new view should pop up that allows you to search for the library and add it to your project.

On the Project Navigator, click on the framework that was just added and verify the Target Membership is correct.
